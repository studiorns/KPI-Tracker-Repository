Market Performance

Market Insights

India and KSA are the top performing markets across all metrics, with India showing the highest Awareness (95.2%) and KSA leading in Intent (48.1%). China remains the most challenging market with the lowest scores across all metrics, though it shows consistent improvement year-over-year.

Market Quadrant Analysis

This analysis uses carefully calibrated midpoints (Performance = 52.0%, Growth = 1.5%) to position markets in quadrants based on their performance and growth metrics. Performance is calculated as the average of Awareness, Consideration, and Intent metrics, while Growth represents the average year-over-year change in these same metrics. The chart now displays Performance on the X-axis and Growth on the Y-axis for better visualization on mobile devices.

Target Insights

The brand health metrics have established targets through 2030. Awareness is targeted to reach 86.5% by 2030, while Familiarity has the most ambitious growth target from 55.2% to 62.2%.
